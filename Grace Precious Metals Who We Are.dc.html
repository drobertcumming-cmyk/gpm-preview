Who we are

Built by a pastor, an accountant, and a marketing executive — for people the industry stopped serving honestly.

Grace Precious Metals exists because three people independently reached the same conclusion about an industry they were each, in their own way, working inside. Below: who we are, what we built, and why we built it the way we did.

Proverbs 11:1 — the anchor

“A false balance is an abomination to the Lord, but a just weight is His delight.”

The operational principle

The verse is not decorative. It is how the business is organised.

The just weight is what the customer is charged. Ours is visible — the number is published. Accurate — what is published is what is charged. Constant — it does not move between customers.

VisibleThe published spread is the visible weight.
AccurateThe salaried-expert model protects the accuracy of the weight.
ConstantThe bullion-only catalogue protects its constancy.

The operational architecture.
