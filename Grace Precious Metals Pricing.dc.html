Spread applies to standard IRS-eligible bullion (Gold Eagles, Gold Maple Leafs, qualifying bars). Custodian and depository fees are third-party charges billed separately — sample figures below. Published spread subject to periodic review.

Why this number is on this page

Because a just weight is His delight.

Most Gold IRA companies do not publish their spread. They quote it on the phone, after they have your attention and your time. We believe that is backwards. If you are entrusting your retirement savings to a company, you deserve to know the cost before you speak to anyone.

The pricing below is what every saver pays. There is no tiered pricing, no volume discount for larger rollovers, and no hidden schedule for smaller ones. The spread is the spread.

Proverbs 11:1

“A false balance is an abomination to the Lord, but a just weight is His delight.”

The weight is what you are charged. Ours is visible, accurate, and constant.

Main commitments

The structure behind the number.

01

Published spread, 11.1% all-in

Published on this page, not quoted on the phone. It includes every cost of entry — no admin fee, no setup fee, no annual account fee. The number you see is the number you pay.

02

Buyback at wholesale, never below

When you decide to sell, Grace buys back your bullion at our wholesale acquisition price. No exit spread, no liquidation fee, no markdown. Your gold is worth what the market says it is worth.

03

Standard IRS-eligible bullion only

American Gold Eagles, Canadian Gold Maple Leafs, and LBMA-approved bars. No numismatic coins, no “exclusive” products, no inflated premiums on rare or collectible items.

04

Salaried experts. No commission.

Our experts are salaried employees. They do not earn commission on your purchase. Their job is to answer your questions — not to close a sale.

How we get paid

The spread is our revenue.

Grace earns revenue from the spread between the price we pay for bullion and the price we charge you. That spread is 11.1%, applied once, at the time of purchase. We earn no ongoing fees, no commission on custodian or depository charges, and no revenue when you sell.

If the economics ever require raising the spread, we will publish the new figure on this page before it takes effect. No such change is planned.

What we don’t charge

No admin fee. No setup fee. No exit fee.

Many Gold IRA companies layer fees on top of their spread: account setup fees, annual administration fees, wire transfer fees, liquidation fees. Grace charges none of these. The 11.1% spread is the complete cost of doing business with us.

Your IRA will carry two third-party charges that are not set by Grace and are billed directly by the custodian and depository — shown alongside.
